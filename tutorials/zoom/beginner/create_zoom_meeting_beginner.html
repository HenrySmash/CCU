Zoom - Create Meeting

  • Step 1 - Open your Zoom app on your desktop and click Sign In.
  • Step 2 - Log in using the E-mail and password that you have created, or with Google(Gmail), Facebook, or Login with SSO.
  • Step 3 - Click the downward arrow and select Start with video, then click New Meeting to start an instant meeting.
