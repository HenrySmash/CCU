Meet - Record Meeting

  • Step 1 - Open Meet and start or join a meeting.
  • Step 2 - Click More and then Record meeting.
  • Step 3 - Wait for the recording to start. Other participants are notified when the recording starts or stops.
  • Step 4 - Click More and then Stop recording when you finish.
  • Step 5 - Click Stop recording again to verify.
  • Step 6 - Wait for the recording file to be generated and saved to the meeting organizerâ€™s My Drive > Meet Recordings folder. An email with the recording link is also sent to the meeting organizer and the person who started the recording.
